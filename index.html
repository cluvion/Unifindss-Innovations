Understanding Unifindss

Unifindss Innovations Pvt Ltd is a fast-growing EdTech startup based in Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh — founded with a clear and powerful mission: to bridge the massive gap between aspiring students and the opportunities they truly deserve.

The platform serves as a one-stop destination for students across India to discover colleges, connect with mentors, and unlock career opportunities. In a country where hundreds of millions of students struggle with fragmented, unreliable information about higher education, Unifindss fills a critical void.

From finding the right university based on course, budget, and location — to getting personalised mentorship from industry professionals — Unifindss empowers students to make confident, informed decisions about their future without the confusion and anxiety that usually comes with it.

Founded in April 2026 and already building momentum, the company is at an exciting inflection point. A strong product, a massive untapped audience, and zero dominant brand in the space. This is the perfect moment to build brand authority through social media.

We don't just post content. We engineer a deliberate brand narrative that positions Unifindss as the most trusted companion in a student's journey — from confused Class 11 student to confident college goer.

The result is a compounding flywheel — content attracts followers, ads convert them, platform experience retains them, and happy users become brand ambassadors.

Paid Advertising

Meta Ads Deep Strategy

Organic reach builds trust and community. Paid ads pour fuel on the fire — driving measurable sign-ups, leads, and platform registrations at scale, fast.

01
Pixel Setup & Audience Research

We install and configure the Meta Pixel on unifindss.com to track every visitor action. We then build custom audience profiles — separating students, parents, and mentors with detailed interest-based, behavioural, and demographic targeting.

02
Campaign Architecture

Separate campaigns are built for Awareness, Consideration, and Conversion objectives. Each campaign has dedicated ad sets per audience segment to isolate performance data and prevent budget waste.

03
Creative Testing (A/B)

Multiple ad creatives are tested simultaneously — different video thumbnails, headline angles, and CTA copy variations. Data determines which creative scales; underperformers are cut within the first week.

04
Retargeting Funnels

Warm audiences who visited unifindss.com or engaged with social content are retargeted with conversion-specific ads — sign-up prompts, free mentorship offers, or powerful testimonial-led creatives.

05
Optimisation & Scaling

Weekly budget reallocation towards best-performing ad sets. Ongoing creative refresh to combat ad fatigue. Detailed reporting on CPL, CTR, ROAS, and user growth metrics every reporting cycle.
