What Performance Marketing Is

Performance marketing means you only pay for results. We set up, manage, and optimise paid ad campaigns across Google (YouTube) and Meta (Instagram + Facebook) — tracking every click, cost-per-lead, and conversion so every rupee of ad spend is accountable.

Our job is campaign strategy, setup, targeting, bidding, ad copy guidance, pixel tracking, and continuous optimisation. Your team's job is producing the actual video and image creatives.

🎯

What This Means for Unifindss

Right now, thousands of students in India are searching Google for "best colleges for commerce" or watching YouTube videos about JEE prep. We put Unifindss directly in front of them at the exact moment they're looking — and on Meta, we find students and parents who don't know Unifindss exists yet.

The result: more sign-ups on unifindss.com, more mentorship bookings, more platform users — all tracked and reported clearly.

Our Process

How We Run Your Campaigns

A clear, repeatable monthly process — from setup to reporting. No black boxes. You always know exactly what's happening and why.

01

Tracking & Pixel Setup

We install and configure Google Tag Manager, the Meta Pixel, and Google Analytics 4 on unifindss.com. Every important action — sign-up, form fill, page visit, button click — is tracked as a conversion event. This is the foundation everything else is built on. No tracking = no data = no optimisation.

One-Time Setup
02

Audience & Keyword Research

We identify the exact search terms students and parents use when looking for college guidance, mentorship, and career planning content. For Meta, we build detailed audience profiles — age, interests, location, behaviour. Separate audiences for students (15–22), parents, and aspiring mentors across India.

Month 1 Ongoing Refinement
03

Content Ideas & Brief Delivery

We provide your team with specific, data-backed content briefs — what to film, what message to lead with, what hook to use in the first 5 seconds, what text overlay to add. Your team creates the content. We tell them exactly what will perform based on platform algorithms and competitor research.

Creative Direction
04

Campaign Setup & Launch

We build the full campaign architecture — ad groups, bidding strategies, targeting layers, placement selection, budget allocation. On Google, we structure Search, YouTube In-Stream, and Display campaigns. On Meta, we run a full three-stage funnel: Awareness → Consideration → Conversion.

Live in Week 2–3
05

Weekly Optimisation

Every week: bid adjustments, negative keyword additions, audience exclusions, pausing underperforming ads, scaling what's working, creative rotation to avoid ad fatigue. Performance marketing requires constant hands-on management — not set-and-forget.

Every Week
06

Monthly Reporting & Strategy Review

A clean, clear monthly report showing: total spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, cost-per-lead, ROAS, and platform sign-ups driven. Alongside the numbers, we include a written strategy note for the next month — what we're changing, testing, and scaling.
